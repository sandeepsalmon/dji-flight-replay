Fly it again.

Drop a DJI video. The flight plays back across the original footage, a satellite map, and a 3D chase camera, all in sync.

Older drone or no telemetry in the file? Drop the matching .SRT next to the video (DJI Fly → gear → Camera → turn on Video Subtitles), or run python3 scripts/extract_srt.py path/to/folder once.

Files stay on your machine. The page is static. Nothing gets uploaded.
